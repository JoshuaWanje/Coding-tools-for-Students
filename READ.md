## Notion

**Notion** is a powerful, all-in-one productivity application designed to organize your thoughts, tasks, and projects in a single workspace. It combines the features of notes, spreadsheets, calendars, and databases so you don't have to switch between multiple apps to manage your information.

### Why Students Should Use It
- It is highly customizable, allowing you to design pages that match exactly how your brain works.
- It supports full Markdown formatting so you can write clean, organized notes quickly.
- It includes powerful databases to track class schedules, assignment due dates, and project milestones.
- It allows you to link pages inside other pages, making it easy to build a personal knowledge base.

### How to Get Started
1. Create a free account at [notion.so](https://www.notion.so/).
2. Browse the built-in template gallery to find a student dashboard or personal planner that fits your needs.
3. Use the `/` command on your keyboard to instantly add text, headers, checkboxes, or images to your page.
